PointDrag

Description:
A draggable object with optional gravity. Emits signals for drag events.

Properties:

  • gravity_enabled: bool — If true, gravity is applied when not dragging.
  • gravity_velocity: float — Gravity strength.

Signals:

  • drag_started — Emitted when dragging starts.
  • drag_moved(new_position: Vector2) — Emitted as the object is dragged.
  • drag_ended — Emitted when dragging ends.

Usage:

  • Add as a child of your scene.
  • Add a CollisionShape2D or CollisionPolygon2D as a child.
  • Configure properties in the Inspector.
