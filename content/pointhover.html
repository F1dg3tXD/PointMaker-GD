PointHover

Description:
Detects when the mouse hovers over the area and can trigger sounds or animations.

Properties:

  • use_own_collision: bool — If true, uses its own collision shape; otherwise, inherits from parent.
  • stop_animation_on_exit: bool — If true, stops the animation when the mouse leaves.
  • sounds: Array[AudioStream] — Sounds to play on hover.
  • animation_player_path: NodePath — Path to an AnimationPlayer node.
  • animation_name: String — Name of the animation to play.

Signals:

  • hover_started — Emitted when the mouse enters the area.
  • hover_triggered — Emitted when hover actions are triggered.
  • hover_ended — Emitted when the mouse leaves the area.

Usage:

  • Add as a child of your scene.
  • Add a CollisionShape2D or CollisionPolygon2D as a child.
  • Configure properties in the Inspector.
