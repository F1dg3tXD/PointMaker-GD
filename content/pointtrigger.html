PointTrigger

Description:
Triggers sounds, animations, or scene changes when a body enters the area or when clicked with the mouse.

Properties:

  • sounds: Array[AudioStream] — List of sounds to play when triggered.
  • animation_player_path: NodePath — Path to an AnimationPlayer node to play an animation.
  • animation_name: String — Name of the animation to play.
  • scene_to_load: String — Path to a .tscn scene to load when triggered.
  • trigger_once: bool — If true, can only be triggered once.

Signals:

  • trigger_started — Emitted when the trigger is activated (by body enter or mouse click).
  • sounds_played — Emitted after all sounds have been played.
  • animation_played(name: String) — Emitted after the animation is played.
  • scene_changed(path: String) — Emitted after the scene is changed.

Usage:

  • Add as a child of your scene.
  • Add a CollisionShape2D or CollisionPolygon2D as a child.
  • Configure properties in the Inspector.
  • Triggers on body enter or mouse click.
